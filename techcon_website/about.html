Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in a converted warehouse in San Francisco. Founded by tech pioneers Dr. Alan Turing and Sarah Chen, the conference was born from a desire to create a space where innovators could share ideas freely and collaborate on solving the world's biggest technological challenges.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has grown exponentially, expanding to multiple continents and attracting over 50,000 annual attendees. What started as a niche event for Silicon Valley insiders has transformed into the world's premier technology conference, influencing global tech trends and launching countless innovations.

Key milestones in our journey include the introduction of the Innovation Awards in 2015, the launch of our Global Impact initiative in 2018 focusing on sustainable technology, and our first fully hybrid conference format in 2021 that reached participants in 156 countries.

Our Mission

At TechCon 2024, our mission is to accelerate technological innovation for the betterment of humanity while fostering an inclusive, collaborative community of forward-thinkers. We believe technology should serve everyone, everywhere.

Our core principles guide everything we do:

Innovation with Purpose: We showcase technologies that solve real-world problems, from climate change to healthcare accessibility.

Knowledge Sharing: We break down silos between industries, academia, and startups to accelerate breakthroughs.

Diversity & Inclusion: We actively work to amplify underrepresented voices in technology, ensuring diverse perspectives shape our future.

Sustainability: We promote and celebrate technologies that advance environmental sustainability and social responsibility.

Accessibility: We strive to make cutting-edge technological knowledge accessible to all through scholarships, free online resources, and community outreach programs.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential minds in technology. Here are a few notable speakers from previous years:

Dr. Maya Rodriguez

TechCon 2023 Keynote Speaker

Founder of Ethical AI Labs and former Google AI Ethics Lead. Dr. Rodriguez's 2023 talk "Algorithmic Justice: Building Fair AI Systems" sparked global conversations about responsible AI development and led to the formation of the Global AI Ethics Consortium.

James K. Lee

TechCon 2022 Featured Speaker

CEO of QuantumLeap Technologies and MIT professor. Lee's groundbreaking quantum encryption demonstration at TechCon 2022 revolutionized data security protocols and earned him the TechCon Innovation Award for Most Disruptive Technology.

Priya Sharma

TechCon 2021 Workshop Leader

Former White House Cybersecurity Advisor and founder of ShieldNet Solutions. Sharma's interactive cybersecurity workshops at TechCon have trained over 5,000 professionals in advanced threat detection and prevention techniques.

Marcus Johnson

TechCon 2020 Panel Moderator

Founder of GreenTech Ventures and UN Technology Ambassador. Johnson's passionate advocacy for sustainable technology at TechCon 2020 inspired the launch of 42 clean tech startups and mobilized $200M in green technology investments.
